What We Do

Citeplasm aims to be a best-in-class research and collaboration tool for academic professionals and students.

Built on Scholarship Graph™

The core - and coolest - feature of Citeplasm is Knowledge Graph, an inter-connected web of reliable informaton and data from governmental and academic organizations throughout the world.

See a demo » Read the Docs »

Citeplasm leverages our Linked Open Data Initiative as well as the open data projects of governmental and academic organizations across the globe to provide fully-integrated access to mountains of articles and a sea of data - all organized, all in one place, all reliable.

Though the Internet is largely a web of documents, the next paradigm is a browseable, semantically-linked web of data. Learn how Emergenesis is linking millions of documents into the Semantic Web.
